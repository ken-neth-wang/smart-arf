BPG Protocol

Benzathine Penicillin G — 5-Step Protocol

Quick reference for administering BPG to suspected ARF patients. Detailed steps and reference photos will be added.

1
Verify indication & allergy history
Confirm ARF suspicion. Ask about penicillin allergy — never administer if history of severe reaction.
2
Prepare BPG injection
Reconstitute according to weight-based dosing (≤27 kg: 600,000 IU; >27 kg: 1.2 million IU). [Photo to be added]
3
Administer deep IM injection
Inject into upper outer quadrant of gluteus or vastus lateralis (children). Use Z-track technique. [Photo to be added]
4
Observe patient for 30 minutes
Monitor for anaphylaxis or other reactions before discharge.
5
Schedule follow-up dose
Repeat every 3–4 weeks. Provide patient with referral code & written schedule.
